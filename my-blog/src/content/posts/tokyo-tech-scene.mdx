    # The Tokyo Tech Scene: A Developer Perspective

    Moving to Tokyo was one of the best decisions I've made. The city's blend of tradition and cutting-edge technology creates a unique environment for developers.

    ## The Japanese Tech Industry

    Japan's tech scene is often underestimated in the West. Companies like:
    - **Sony** - Still innovating in imaging and gaming
    - **Rakuten** - A tech powerhouse with 1.5B+ users
    - **Mercari** - Leading the二手 (secondhand) economy
    - **Preferred Networks** - Pushing AI boundaries

    And startups are booming. The government is actively pushing for digital transformation.

    ## Work Culture

    Unlike the stereotype, modern Japanese tech companies are adapting:

    - **Work-life balance** is improving, especially in startups
    - **English** is increasingly common, especially in foreign companies
    - **Remote work** became normalized post-COVID
    - **Meritocracy** is gaining ground over seniority-based promotion

    ### What's Still Different

    - **Meetings** - More formal, often with detailed pre-materials
    - **Documentation** - Extremely thorough (sometimes too thorough)
    - **Quality focus** - Strong emphasis on stability and testing
    - **Hierarchy** - Still exists, but less rigid in tech

    ## Job Market for Foreign Developers

    Good news:
    - Demand far exceeds supply for skilled engineers
    - English-friendly companies are common in Tokyo
    - Salaries are competitive (though not Silicon Valley level)
    - Visa sponsorship is relatively straightforward

    Challenges:
    - Japanese language helps but isn't always required
    - Cultural fitting can be an adjustment
    - Big companies may still prefer Japanese-speaking candidates

    ## Living in Tokyo as a Developer

    The quality of life is exceptional:
    - Public transport is world-class
    - Food scene is incredible
    - Safety is outstanding
    - Tech meetups and communities are active

    ### My Recommendation

    If you're a developer interested in Asia, Tokyo is a fantastic choice. Start with English-friendly companies, learn Japanese gradually, and embrace the culture.

    The combination of interesting work, great living conditions, and unique cultural experience makes it worth considering.
